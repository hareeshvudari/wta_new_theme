WTA is a New Age User-Centric UI/UX Design & Dev Studio dedicated to create significant applications through impactful user experience for startups and MNCs.Our office is a playground for technology and innovation where an intimate group of hand picked tech-nerds and creative cool-heads help find the sweet spot for your product and end-user to fit into the digital marketplace flawlessly.

WTA considers each project as an opportunity to leave a unique mark on the Visual & Technical anthropology of the digital world. We bring the client & User to centre-stage and hope to establish a lasting relationship through problem-solving, attention to detail, functionality and simplified User Experience. With various clients from London, Florida, Belgium, New York, San Francisco and more, WTA creates for a global audience. We are now international !
